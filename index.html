FAQ

How does refund work?
Full refund available if the group is not completed within 24 hours. No cancellation after group completion.
How to change pickup point?
Before group completion, captains can edit pickup info in "Initiated Groups". After completion, contact the captain.
Quality issues?
Report within 24 hours of pickup with photos. Contact captain or support for assistance.
How to become a captain?
Click "Initiate" on home page to start. After 3 successful groups, upgrade to Senior Captain.
